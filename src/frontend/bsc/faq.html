Hive BEP20
The gateway between HIVE and Binance Smart Chain.
Hive BEP20 (BHIVE) is an Binanace Smart Chain BEP20 token that represents HIVE. Each BHIVE is backed by real HIVE and can be converted back to HIVE at any time.
To receive BHIVE, send HIVE to @wrapped-hive-bsc with your BSC address as a memo.
To receive HIVE, enter your Hive username into "BHIVE to HIVE" form (front page). You need to have MetaMask installed. Alternatively, you can use your favourite method of interacting with smart contract (BscScan.com/direct RPC calls/...) and call convert method with parameters: amountOfTokens, username
