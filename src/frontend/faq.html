Wrapped Hive
Gateway between HIVE and ETH.
Wrapped Hive (WHIVE) is an Ethereum ERC20 token that represents HIVE. Each WHIVE is backed by real HIVE and can be converted back to HIVE at any time.
To receive WHIVE, send HIVE to @wrapped-hive with your Ethereum address as a memo.
To receive HIVE, enter your Hive username into "WHIVE to HIVE" form (front page). You will receive deposit details, so send any amount of WHIVE to the given address. Be aware that the address will expire in 7 days, so you will need to generate a new address every week.
To reduce page loading speed, our data is calculated once per hour and might not be 100% up to date.
